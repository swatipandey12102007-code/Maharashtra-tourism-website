Welcome to Maharashtra Tourism

Maharashtra Tourism is your gateway to one of India’s most culturally rich and geographically diverse states. From the bustling streets of Mumbai to the tranquil backwaters of Konkan, we invite you to explore the heart of India.

Our mission is to promote sustainable tourism, preserve heritage, and provide unforgettable experiences. Whether you're a history buff, nature lover, or spiritual seeker, Maharashtra offers something for everyone.

We work closely with local communities, artisans, and eco-tourism initiatives to ensure every journey supports the people and places that make Maharashtra special.

Why Visit Maharashtra?

The third largest state and the second most populous state in India - Maharashtra - is brimming with an array of colorful shrines; a remarkable collection of caves; golden beaches lined with swaying palm trees; historic hotspots with strong past connection; and oodles of emerald hill stations under the canopy of lush western ghats.

  • UNESCO World Heritage Sites like Ajanta & Ellora
  • Vibrant festivals including Ganesh Chaturthi and Gudi Padwa
  • Delicious cuisine from spicy Misal Pav to coastal seafood
  • Historic forts, serene temples, and lush hill stations
